Meet Our Leadership

Our dedicated team of professionals brings decades of experience to ensure the highest quality of care for our clients.

Andrew Nimely, MBA

Andrew Nimely has over 10 years of healthcare administration experience, specializing in quality and compliance. Anne Mulama, MSN, RN

Anne Mulama serves as Chief Executive Officer and Director of Clinical Services. She brings 20 years of diverse nursing experience across leading healthcare institutions, including The Ohio State University, Cleveland Clinic, OhioHealth, Mount Carmel Health System, and Alta Bates Summit Medical in San Francisco. With a Master's in Nursing, Anne's clinical expertise ensures that every patient receives the highest standard of care.
